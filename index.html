How to use Animedoro

It's like Pomodoro, but you actually enjoy the breaks.
Instead of the regular 25 + 5 Pomodoro technique,
Animedoro uses 40 ~ 60 minutes and 20+ minutes timers
you work at your own pace for 40 to 60 minutes
then watch an episode of anime for 20+ minutes.
A full cycle is called an Animedoro.
